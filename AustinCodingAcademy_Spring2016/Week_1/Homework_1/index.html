About Us

Tim is a lifetime lover of all animals of both land and sea. At 16 he channeled his passion into preserving kills for friends and family. Through trial, error, and a few unfortunate mounts, Tim perfected his skills in the age old art of taxidermy. Tim takes pride in being the only taxidermist in the tri state area that will take on any animal project, land, sea, or sky, wild or domestic.
