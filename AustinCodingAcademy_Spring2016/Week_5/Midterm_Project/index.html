About Charlie

University of California, Santa Barbara BFA graduate, Charlie Faith is a Los Angeles based actor, comedian, writer, and producer. Drawn to the stage from a young age, Charlie has spent years honing his craft. Recently becoming a memeber of The Groundlings Sunday Company Charlie has been able to persue his love of improv and sketch comedy. As a memeber of the production team at Collab Creators, Charlie has done extensive work one the other side of the lense as well, traveling throughout the US porducing high quality content.
